Volume Control

Purpose

  • Regulate the volume and play/pause per keyboaard

How To

  • Ctrl + Numpad/: Volume Down
  • Ctrl + Numpad*: Volume Up
  • Numpad0 and Numpad+: Mute
  • Numpad.: Pause
  • Ctrl + Numpad3: Stop
  • Ctrl + Numpad0: Next Song
  • Ctrl + Numpad1: Previous Song
