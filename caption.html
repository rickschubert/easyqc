Caption Comparer

Purpose

  • Quickly open files side by side to compare them next to another
  • This is especially useful for Caption QC, where you have a large amount of files to quickly view side by side

How To

  • Open two explorer windows with the files you want to compare against each other (and open them side by side)
  • "Highlight" in the explorer both files you want to open (single click on them, without opening)
  • Alt + W opens both files and puts them in both monitors in full screen
  • Alt + Q closes all opened PDF files (the ones you just opened) and opens the next PDF files from your two explorer windows side by side
